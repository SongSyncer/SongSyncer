// What it does

Everything in sync,
nothing missed.

🎵

Automatic Detection

Drop a Spotify or YouTube link in your designated Discord channel and Song Syncer picks it up instantly — no commands needed.

🔄

Cross-Platform Sync

Spotify links get added to your YouTube playlist and vice versa. One link, both platforms, zero effort.

🚫

Duplicate Prevention

Already in the playlist? Song Syncer knows. No doubles, no clutter — just a clean, growing library.

↩️

Undo Support

Added something by mistake? React to undo the addition without digging through your playlists manually.

📋

Activity Logging

Every addition is logged with the track name, source, and who shared it — full visibility into your playlist history.

❤️

Health Monitoring

Built-in health endpoint lets you verify the bot is up and running at any time from any network.

// How it works

Simple by design.

01

Share a link in Discord

Paste any Spotify track or YouTube video URL into your configured music thread. That's the whole input.

02

Song Syncer detects it

The bot watches the channel in real time and immediately processes any recognized music link.

03

Cross-platform lookup

The track is searched on the other platform — Spotify finds the YouTube equivalent and vice versa.

04

Both playlists updated

The track is added to your Spotify playlist and your YouTube playlist simultaneously. Duplicates are skipped automatically.
